Who We Help

Finance support for businesses that need clarity, structure and fewer missed deadlines.

We support owner-managed companies, self-employed professionals and growing SMEs with bookkeeping, VAT, payroll, compliance and practical finance operations.

Businesses We Work Well With

Owner-Managed Limited Companies

Directors who need reliable bookkeeping, VAT, payroll and year-end support without building an internal finance team.

Self-Employed Professionals

Consultants, clinicians, creatives and independent professionals who want their income, expenses and tax records kept clear.

Cafés, Retail & Local Services

Busy local businesses that need practical support with payroll, VAT, supplier payments, sales records and day-to-day finance admin.

Growing SMEs

Companies moving beyond basic bookkeeping and needing management accounts, forecasts, cash flow tracking and better reporting.

International Founders in the UK

Founders who operate across more than one market and need UK finance processes explained and managed in a clear, practical way.

Businesses Needing a Clean-Up

Businesses with historic bookkeeping gaps, unclear director transactions, overdue records or finance processes that need bringing under control.

How Support Usually Starts

Different businesses need different levels of finance support.

Some clients come to us for catch-up work. Others need monthly accounting support, management accounts or a more structured finance function.

01

Catch-Up & Clean-Up

For businesses with gaps in bookkeeping, unclear records, overdue admin or accounts that need bringing back into order.

02

Monthly Accounting Support

For businesses that want regular bookkeeping, VAT, payroll, reconciliations and compliance support handled properly each month.

03

Finance Partner Support

For growing SMEs that need management accounts, cash flow insight, reporting and practical finance operations support.
