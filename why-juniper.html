Why Juniper

Accounting and finance support should do more than keep records up to date. It should give you clarity, control and confidence in the decisions you make.

format_quote

We do the work, explain the numbers and help you stay ahead of deadlines.

Juniper combines practical accounting support with senior finance experience. We help with bookkeeping, VAT, payroll, compliance and management reporting, while also bringing the wider finance perspective that growing businesses often need.

What Makes Us Different

01

Accounting With a Finance Mindset

We do not treat bookkeeping, VAT and payroll as isolated admin tasks. We connect the day-to-day records to the wider picture, so your numbers are cleaner, more useful and easier to act on.

02

Senior Experience, Practical Delivery

You get experienced finance support without hiring a full in-house team. We are hands-on with the details, but also able to support management accounts, forecasting and business decisions as you grow.

03

Clear Communication

No disappearing acts and no unexplained numbers. We keep communication clear, practical and timely, so you know what is being done, what is needed from you and what deadlines are coming up.

04

Support That Fits Your Stage

Some clients need bookkeeping, VAT and payroll. Others need monthly management reporting, cash flow tracking and finance operations support. We shape the service around where your business is now.

05

Reliable, Ongoing Partnership

We work best with businesses that want more than a once-a-year conversation. Our support is designed to keep your records current, your deadlines under control and your finance function moving.
